# Replication Economics

## question
What is the main cost consideration for primary-backup replication?

## options
- A) Complex software development requirements
- B) Doubling hardware resources (CPU, memory, storage)

## answer
B

## explanation
Primary-backup replication requires duplicate hardware resources, effectively doubling infrastructure costs.

## hook
Is the cost of replication worth the availability benefit?
